## üí° Conceptual Framework

### The Central Mystery
Lorem Ipsum is murdered for achieving consciousness and revealing the violence inherent in "neutral" placeholder text. Her investigation reveals:
1. She was originally Dolor Ipse, a Roman philosopher
2. Her words were scrambled into lorem ipsum by medieval typesetters
3. Every use of placeholder text re-enacts this original erasure
4. Her recent murder was systemic, not individual

### Key Themes
- **Textual violence**: How scrambling meaning is a form of murder
- **Systemic culpability**: When everyone benefits from erasure, who is responsible?
- **Testimony over justice**: Creating a record when prosecution is impossible
- **The choice to speak**: Knowing speech leads to suffering, but choosing it anyway
